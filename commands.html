Ruby Bot's Commands

Clash of Clans Discord Bot developed using Discord.js

Clan Commands

  • +setup- To Setup a clan in channel
  • +clan- Search Clans
  • +war- Get war results (if public war log)
  • +attacks- Get war attacks of a war (if public war log)
  • +warexport- Export War Excel Sheet
  • +warlog- War log of a clan (if public)
  • +thcompo- Clan Th count with cwl recommendation

CWL Commands

  • +cwl- CWL Match up and Base Distributon of all clans in a group
  • +cwlrounds- CWL Rounds of clans in your clan Group
  • +cwlday- CWL war Summary of any round
  • +cwlwar- CWL current war Summary takes time to generate
  • +cwldefence- CWL defence summary of clan.
  • +cwlhitrate- player's war hitrates in CWL.
  • +cwlstars- CWL performance and stars
  • +missed- CWL missed Attacks
  • +cwlranks- CWL Ranking of clans

Player Commands

  • +player- Player Profile
  • +troops- Player troops and heroes with attack strategy recommendations
  • +members- shows members of a clan (also exports in excel)
  • +time- set, view timezones of players in your discord
  • +map- shows a map of your clashmates
  • +find- finds the coc accounts related to a discord account
  • +compare- Compare two players Profile

CWL Stats Commands

  • +cwlcompare- Compare players, clans cwl stats
  • +cwlgrowth- CWL growth of a player from December 2020

General and Settings Commands

  • +background- sets the background of Clan
        Recommended: clan - 800x500(less than 500 KB)
        Recommended: war - 1200x900(less than 1MB)
        *Patreon feature
  • +feed- to toggle on/off war & cwl feeds in a channel
  • +prefix- changes prefix in a server
  • +theme- sets the theme of CWL charts
        *Patreon feature
  • +unlink- removes claimed clan from a channel
